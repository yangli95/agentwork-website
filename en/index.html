Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI Agents really attend meetings for me?
Yes, AgentWork's AI Agents can join various meeting platforms (Zoom, Tencent Meeting, Feishu, etc.), record meeting content in real-time, understand key discussion points, and automatically extract action items. The Agent joins as "XXX's AI Assistant" to ensure transparency.
Is my meeting content secure?
We use enterprise-grade security standards. All meeting content is transmitted and stored with end-to-end encryption. You can choose data storage regions and delete all meeting records at any time. We strictly comply with data protection regulations.
Do I need technical knowledge to use it?
Not at all! AgentWork is designed for regular users with a visual Agent creation interface. You can create your dedicated AI assistant in just 5 minutes. We also provide detailed tutorials and customer support.
Can it integrate with our company systems?
Yes! We support integration with mainstream office software including Feishu, DingTalk, WeChat Work, Slack, Microsoft Teams, etc. Enterprise plans also offer API interfaces and custom integration services.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription anytime in account settings with no fees. After cancellation, you can continue using until the current billing period ends, then automatically downgrade to the free plan.
Is there a free trial period?
Yes! We offer a free plan to experience core features, and paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. During the trial, you can use all features without limits. Pay only when satisfied.
